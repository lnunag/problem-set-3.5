Notable Awards

The Vince Lombardi Trophy

On February 4, 2018, at U.S. Bank Stadium in Minneapolist Minnesota, the
Philadelphia Eaglesplayed the New England Patriots in the SuperBowl LII.
The NFC champions defeated the AFC and defending Super Bowl LI champions,
41-33, to win their first Super Bowl. It was a very exciting day for all
Eagles fans, the first Vince Lombardi trophy to ever be awarded to our team.

In the recent Eagles-Giants Game, the usual 10 awards were handed out.
Here are the ones awarded to the Eagles.

  • The 'Still Great' Award: Carson Wentz
  • The 'Tough Rugged Man' Award: Lane Johnson
  • The 'Is He Done' Award: Jason Peters
  • The 'Rapport' Award: Alshon Jeffery
  • The 'Bully' Award: The Eagles' pass rush
  • The 'Bounce Back' Award: The Eagles' secondary
  • The 'Beast of the Least' Award: The Eagles
